Elizabeth C. Crites

My current research interests are in public-key cryptography and include the following:

  • constructing novel signature schemes
  • improving security for multi-party signature schemes
  • developing distributed key generation protocols
  • building simple and efficient anonymous credential systems
  • designing privacy-preserving cryptographic primitives
